The Circuits No Means No course is a pedagogical initiative aimed at training workers and supervisors in establishments, nightclubs, restaurants, and bars under Law 14.786/2023, which establishes the “No Means No” protocol. The course will be available in a Moodle learning environment, where materials, classes, exercises, etc., will be provided. The course can be accessed at https://circuitos.org.br.
